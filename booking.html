Laundry Checklist Guidelines

  • 1. Separate your delicates, dry-clean-only pieces, and shoes into their own labeled plastic/canvas bags.

Quick Booking FAQs

What if I'm not home during the slot?

No problem! Simply note a secure drop-off location (e.g. concierge, porch box) in the booking address notes.

Can I change my pickup window later?

Yes. Rescheduling or modifying scheduled collections is free and can be managed directly on your user portal dashboard.

Is there a weight minimum?

No minimum load weights are enforced. We charge a flat $5 logistics routing fee for all residential pickups.
