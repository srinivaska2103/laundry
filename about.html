Founded to Modernize Your Laundry Day

FreshFold Laundry began in 2011 with a simple realization: laundry is an archaic, time-consuming chore that hasn't adapted to modern schedules. We wanted to build a service that respects your time, values delicate fabrics, and protects the environment.

Today, we are proud to serve over 10,000 active residential and commercial clients across 35 pickup locations. Every bag is sorted individually and washed in filtered soft water utilizing biodegradable solvents.

Our Mission

To automate laundry day for families and businesses so they can focus on their primary callings.

Our Vision

To become the national standard for carbon-neutral dry cleaning and automated home pickup services.

Our Values

Fabric integrity, transparent pricing parameters, absolute punctuality, and eco-responsibility pickup services.

Advanced Engineering

Our Processing Facility Standards

We invest in premium garment restoration systems that replace harsh dry cleaning chemistry with gentle, advanced extraction logic.

High-G Wash Extractors

Programmable extraction cycles rotate at high speeds to safely extract moisture, reducing gas-dry cycles and extending fabric lifespans.

Extract rating Up to 450 G-Force

Ozone Sanitization Cabinets

Infused cold-water ozone cycles sanitize fabrics dynamically, destroying odor bacteria and mold spores without requiring high temperature wash damage.

Sanitize rate 99.9% Microbial reduction

De-Mineralized Soft Water

Multi-stage reverse osmosis filtration strips hard magnesium and calcium minerals, keeping colors bright and fibers soft.

Purity rating < 10 ppm TDS
